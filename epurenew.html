OVERVIEW

Introduction

épuré is an e-commerce platform that brings modern furniture to contemporary homes. The brand emphasizes timeless design and craftsmanship offering a curated selection of sleek, staple pieces.

Research

Competitive Analysis

After analyzing the brand identities of competing platforms, I was inspired to take a different approach for the development of épuré’s mobile app. I noticed that similar brands leaned heavily into minimalism and although creates a sleek, modern look, it felt quite bland.

BRANDING

Elements

Rounded shapes were one of the first ideas that came to mind because it helps to create a more inviting environment and aligning with this overarching, contemporary theme I was going for with Épuré’s mobile app and identity. Rounded elements help to soften the overall design making it more approachable.

To complement this, I opted for neutral and earthy tones for the color palette. Not only adding into the approachable feel but also reinforces a more natural, calming environment.

Logo

After a few hand-sketched ideas, I used Adobe Illustrator to finalize the design where I utilized tools like Pathfinder, Guides, and Grids to ensure perfect symmetry and alignment within the logo. Further building into the brand identity, I finalized the logo design with these series of curved lines symbolizing arches and doorways to help reflect the welcoming nature of the brand.

Design

Illustrations

A primary user flow was created that maps out the main route of the typical user - from sign-up to discovery, navigation and tracking progress. This exercise ensured that the main interaction points for the app were covered and taken into account to remove any obstacles that the users may face. It also creates clear routes and decision points to help me create a more user-friendly app.

Reflection

Designing the website mock-up for Virtus has been an amazing learning experience, reinforcing the importance of content writing, UX strategies, and branding differentiation. While this project mainly focused on content writing for targeted audiences, it also emphasized the need to solve common design challenges faced by fitness brands/businesses.
