Amacon

Overview

A Legacy, Modernized

This rebranding concept for Amacon aims to elevate the company’s visual and digital identity to better match its reputation as a leader in the real estate development and construction industry. The goal of the project was to introduce a modern, cohesive, and sophisticated brand system that reflects the company’s stature.

Reflection

The rebranding concept for Amacon was an amazing learning experience and a great opportunity to refine my skills in branding. Overall, it allowed me to strengthen my technical expertise in Adobe Creative Suite while focusing on creating a cohesive visual identity across various applications.

Though I am happy with the results, I would've enjoyed expanding on the project further by developing more real-worl scenarios for this new identity.

Moving forward, this project allowed me to think more critically about brand design. I aim to apply the skills I've gained to my future projects, ensuring each one balances creativity and real-world relevance.
