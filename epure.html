OVERVIEW

Introduction

épuré is an e-commerce platform that brings modern furniture to contemporary homes. The brand emphasizes timeless design and craftsmanship offering a curated selection of sleek, staple pieces.

Research

Competitive Analysis

After analyzing the brand identities of competing platforms, I was inspired to take a different approach for the development of épuré’s mobile app. I noticed that similar brands leaned heavily into minimalism and although creates a sleek, modern look, it felt quite bland.

BRANDING

Elements

Rounded shapes were one of the first ideas that came to mind because it helps to create a more inviting environment and aligning with this overarching, contemporary theme I was going for with Épuré’s mobile app and identity. Rounded elements help to soften the overall design making it more approachable.

To complement this, I opted for neutral and earthy tones for the color palette. Not only adding into the approachable feel but also reinforces a more natural, calming environment.

Logo

After a few hand-sketched ideas, I used Adobe Illustrator to finalize the design where I utilized tools like Pathfinder, Guides, and Grids to ensure perfect symmetry and alignment within the logo. Further building into the brand identity, I finalized the logo design with these series of curved lines symbolizing arches and doorways to help reflect the welcoming nature of the brand.

Design

Illustrations

Two illustrations were created in Illustrator with a similar approach to the logo and brand design. The first illustration on the home page’s banner introduces a promotion set on a blank beige canvas with a playful living room design. The second, displayed on the payment success screen, consists of dark earthier tones to complement the palette used throughout the mobile app design.

Wireframing

When creating the wireframes of the app in Illustrator as required by the project rubric, I wanted to showcase a few screens of the shopping experience while reflecting the brand’s inviting aesthetic. Each screen was carefully designed with grids and guides in place to ensure precision in the layout and alignment of the elements.

Later on in the project, I recreated the wireframes in Figma to finalize the screens and modernize the approach taken to UI Design.

Reflection

Overall, this project was a rewarding learning experience in app and graphic design, providing me an opportunity to refine my skills in Adobe Illustrator. It taught me the importance of balancing creativity with functionality while addressing design challenges.

While I am proud of the work done for épuré, there’s tons of areas I wished to have improved. Some key things I wished to have included into the development of the project are user research methods and usability testing. Understanding that UX plays a huge role into UI Design, these techniques would have helped épuré to be developed with a user-centric approach and ensure the app meets the needs of its target audience more effectively.
