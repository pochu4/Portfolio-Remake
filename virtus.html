Virtus

Overview

Going Beyond the Gym Floor

Anxiety, Insecurities, Intimidation - All common emotions felt by the average gym go-er, holding them back from achieving their potential.

Virtus is a fictional fitness club designed to create an inclusive, non-intimidating environment.

Role

UX Strategy, UI Design, Content Writing

Software

Figma, Illustrator, InDesign

Timeframe

24 Hours

Purpose, VP, Target Audiences

The purpose of the website is to showcase the wide range of amenities offered and differentiate ourselves from other fitness facilities. Our goal is to guide potential clients to sign-up for a membership by showcasing the benefits of Virtus. As an anti big-box gym, we stand behind authenticity and aim to create a welcoming environment that makes everyone feel supported no matter where they stand in their fitness journey. Throughout the website we want to inspire confidence and show that Virtus is more than just a gym - it’s a community focused on growth.

Research

To kickoff the project and ensure the success of Virtus, I started by conducting research involving analyzing trends in the fitness market, identifying the key competitors, and understanding the needs and preferences of our target audiences. A persona was developed to better understand each of their needs, motivations, and pain points. Additionally, an empathy map was created to help me capture their thoughts and feelings which helped me to tailor the website’s content to two types of users.

This analysis opened doors for ROAM to excel in areas like offline functionality, personalized features, and real-time weather updates. All of my insights gained were then compiled into a user persona, scenario, user journey map and a user flow.

Reflection

Designing the website mock-up for Virtus has been an amazing learning experience. While the project primarily focused on content writing for targeted audiences, it also highlighted the importance of addressing common design problems faced by Virtus' competitors. Being able to identify these pain points and implementing a solution Virtus could further differentiate itself from competitors and help the business stand out.

As I'm quite early on in my career, this project helped me understand the difficulties of tying web design, content creation, and user-centric approaches all together and although I'm proud of the work done for the project, there are numerous areas I'd hope to expand on.
